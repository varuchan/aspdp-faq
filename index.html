Frequently Asked Questions

What is the After School Professional Development Program?
The After School Professional Development Program offers NYC teachers and secretaries the opportunity to take In-Service courses. These courses are credited toward salary differentials, and may be used by teachers towards the NYS 175 hours of Professional Development requirement as long as your principal approves. Paraprofessionals may take these courses toward the NYS 75 hour Professional Development requirement.
May I view the courses before I register?
By clicking on the course code, you link to the course details page which includes a description of the course, dates and times offered, instructor's contact information and payment instructions.
Who may register for the courses?
All NYC DOE teachers, secretaries, paraprofessionals, and NYC Charter School teachers may take “P” In-Service courses for “P” Credit. Paraprofessionals may only use “P” credits toward their NYS Professional Development hours. Guidance counselors may only take Guidance or Special Education courses for In-Service “P” credit. NYC private and parochial school teachers may take a “P” In-Service course to further their own knowledge and may be used towards the NYS 175 hours of Professional Development requirement but no credit will be issued.
